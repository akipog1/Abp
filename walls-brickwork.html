About This Service

Solid Walls, Built
to Stand the Test of Time

Whether you need a retaining wall to level a sloping garden, a boundary wall to define your property, or decorative brickwork to add character — our team builds it right, first time.

At AB Paving & Landscaping, we've been building garden walls and brickwork features across Glasgow and Central Scotland for over 20 years. We use quality materials and traditional craftsmanship to deliver walls that look great and stay solid for decades — no matter what the Scottish weather throws at them.

From a simple garden step to a full retaining wall project, we handle everything from design and groundwork through to the finishing touches.
