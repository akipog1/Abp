About This Service

A Lawn You'll Love.
A Fence Built to Last.

A fresh lawn and a solid, well-installed fence can completely change the feel of your outdoor space. Whether your garden is a bare mud patch or just needs a refresh, our team will have it looking fantastic in no time.

We supply and lay high-quality turf suitable for Scottish conditions, prepare the ground properly and ensure your new lawn has the best possible start. For those who want low maintenance all year round, we also install premium artificial grass that looks natural and stays green whatever the weather.

Our fencing team installs all types of timber fencing — from traditional closeboard panels to decorative picket fencing — using treated timber that stands up to the Scottish climate.
